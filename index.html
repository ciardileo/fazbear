About Us

Ladies and gentlemen, children of all ages, and lovers of thrilling entertainment, welcome to Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, a place where magic, mystery, and mouthwatering pizza converge to create an unforgettable experience. Today, we embark on a journey through the enchanting world of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, where every visit is an adventure.

Founded in 1983, Freddy Fazbear's Pizza has been a beloved institution for generations.

A collaboration between visionary inventors William Afton and Henry Emily, this pizzeria was destined for greatness.

Our Creators

William Afton

William Afton, a brilliant and enigmatic inventor, played a pivotal role in the creation of the animatronic wonders that have become synonymous with Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. His ingenuity knew no bounds, and it was his vision that gave birth to the charming, albeit eerie, robotic characters that would serve as the centerpiece of the restaurant's entertainment. However, Afton's involvement in the pizzeria's history extends far beyond mere engineering. His shadowy presence would soon cast a long, ominous spell over the establishment, as rumors of sinister happenings and unexplained disappearances began to circulate.

Henry Emily

Henry Emily, on the other hand, was the yin to Afton's yang. As a co-founder of the beloved pizzeria, he provided the creative and compassionate touch that brought warmth to the otherwise unsettling animatronic cast. Emily's contributions to the establishment extended beyond its cheerful facade, as he was also a deeply committed father. His dedication to family values and genuine love for children was reflected in the heartwarming stories and laughter that filled the pizzeria during its heyday.
